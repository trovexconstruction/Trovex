TROVEX — GOOGLE INDEXING SETUP

1) Upload all files in this folder to the ROOT of the GitHub repository and replace old files.
2) Open:
   https://trovexconstruction.github.io/Trovex/
   Then press Ctrl + F5.

GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE:
1) Open Google Search Console using the company Gmail.
2) Add a URL-prefix property:
   https://trovexconstruction.github.io/Trovex/
3) Verification method: HTML tag.
4) Google will give you a tag similar to:
   <meta name="google-site-verification" content="TOKEN">
5) Paste it inside <head> in index.html, upload the file, then click Verify.
6) In Sitemaps, submit:
   sitemap.xml
7) Use URL Inspection for:
   https://trovexconstruction.github.io/Trovex/
   Then click Request Indexing.

GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE:
- Create or claim the TROVEX business profile.
- Use the exact same company name, phone, email and address shown on the website.
- Add this website URL:
  https://trovexconstruction.github.io/Trovex/
- Add real project photos, working hours, services and company description.

IMPORTANT:
Google appearance is not instant. Indexing may begin within days, while ranking for “Trovex” can take longer.
A custom domain such as trovexconstruction.com generally looks more professional and is easier to brand.
